My Spending Style

What is the spending style test?

This is a light consumption-tendency test that reflects how you spend. It reveals your style across three axes — how much (spend freely vs. save carefully), how (impulse vs. planned), and on what (experience/trends vs. value/practical). Discover which of the 8 spending styles fits you — light and fun, just like MBTI.

How you spend mirrors who you are

With the same money, one person buys without hesitation what they love, another carefully compares and picks the most practical option, another saves day-to-day and only goes big for a special experience. How you spend quietly reveals your values and lifestyle. Answer honestly about your everyday spending habits and you may meet a side of your consumer self you never noticed. (This test is for fun.)

How it works

Answer 9 simple questions while recalling your everyday spending habits. It takes only about a minute. At the end you will discover your spending style type and a description of your tendency — which of the 8 styles are you? Share the result with friends!
