My Spending Type SBTI

What is the SBTI spending-type test?

SBTI stands for Spending Behavior Type Indicator — a playful test that sorts how you spend into types. What you hesitate over, what you happily open your wallet for — it mirrors everyone’s different spending habits into a handful of types. It’s light, fun content, just like MBTI.

Your spending habits mirror who you are

With the same money, one person spends on experiences, another on things, another saves for the future. Where you put your spending priorities quietly reveals your values and taste. Answer honestly about your everyday shopping habits, and you may meet a spending tendency you never noticed.

How it works

Answer 12 simple questions while recalling your everyday spending habits. It takes only a minute or two. At the end you will meet your spending type with a description, and you can share the result with friends.
