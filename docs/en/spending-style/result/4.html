Save day-to-day, then splurge on the big moment

Shopper, you save diligently day-to-day and then go all-in for the moments that matter. Small everyday expenses? Easy to skip. But when something you really want appears, there's no hesitation. The 'big splurge for me' is itself the motivation to keep saving. To keep 'special moments' from arriving too often, planning your big-spend events for the year in advance keeps things balanced.
