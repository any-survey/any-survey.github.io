My AI Mate Type

What is the AI mate type test?

This is a light test that reflects what kind of presence AI is to you. It reveals your type across two axes — how you treat AI (friendly like a buddy vs. clean like a tool) and how you use it (delegating for results vs. learning to grow). Meet one of four types: Secretary, Sidekick, Friend, or Mentor. Light and fun, just like MBTI.

How you treat AI mirrors who you are

Some people say "thanks" to AI; others type only the request, clean and minimal. Some hand over entire chores; others dig into why an answer came out that way. The same AI becomes a completely different presence for each person. Answer honestly while recalling your everyday AI conversations, and you may discover a relationship you never noticed. (This test is for fun.)

How it works

Answer 10 simple questions while recalling how you usually talk with AI. It takes only about a minute. The questions flow like a chat, and at the end you will discover your AI mate type and a description of your tendency. Share the result with friends — which of the four types is your AI?
