Out of many houses in the world Which house is Perfect for me? A gorgeous city house? A small house in the forest? Through 10 simple questions Let's find out the house I want to live in!

A home reflects who you are

Some people dream of the middle of a city buzzing with people and lights; others long for a small house in the forest where birdsong opens the day. The home you want to live in quietly reveals the kind of days you want to spend and what you hold dear. Answer honestly while picturing the scenes you are drawn to, and you may meet the house that suits you best. (This test is for fun.)

How it works

Answer 10 simple questions while recalling the spaces and moods you are naturally drawn to. It takes only about a minute. At the end you will discover the type of house that suits you and a description of it, and you can share the result with friends. Find out what your ideal home looks like!
