Privacy Policy

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to Any Survey (https://any-survey.github.io, the "Service"). The Service can be used without sign-up or login, and we do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name or email address.

However, cookies and automatically collected usage data may be used to operate the Service, analyze usage statistics, and display advertisements.

2. Information We Collect and Cookies

The Service uses cookies and automatically collected information (browser type, device information, pages visited, access time, etc.) to analyze usage statistics and serve advertisements. This information does not, by itself, directly identify an individual.

3. Google Analytics

The Service uses Google Analytics to analyze visits and usage behavior. Google collects and analyzes Service usage information through cookies. You can opt out of Google Analytics data collection by installing a browser add-on.

  • Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on

4. Google AdSense and Third-Party Advertising Cookies

The Service displays advertisements through Google AdSense. Third-party advertising vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve personalized ads based on your prior visits and other information.

You can disable personalized advertising in Google Ad Settings. For more details on the use of cookies, please refer to the links below.

  • How Google uses cookies in advertising
  • Ad Settings (disable personalized ads)

5. Third-Party Services

The Service uses tools from external providers such as Google for analytics and advertising. Information collected and processed by these providers is subject to each provider’s own privacy policy.

  • Google Privacy Policy

6. Your Choices

You can refuse the storage of cookies or delete already-stored cookies through your web browser settings. However, blocking cookies may limit the use of some features of the Service.

7. Contact

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out through the GitHub issues page below.
