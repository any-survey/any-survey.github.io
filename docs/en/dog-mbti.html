What is the MBTI personality type?

MBTI is a personality indicator that describes people through 16 types. It reads tendencies from four axes: Extraversion(E)·Introversion(I), Sensing(S)·Intuition(N), Thinking(T)·Feeling(F), and Judging(J)·Perceiving(P). This test playfully applies that idea to your own dog.

A dog's character shows on walks and at play

The pup that dashes up wagging its tail to a new friend, the one that freezes at an unfamiliar sound, the one that chases a ball to the very end — every dog likes different things and reacts in its own way. Answer while picturing your dog on walks and at playtime for a result that fits better.

How it works

Answer 12 simple questions while picturing your own dog. It takes only a minute or two. At the end you will meet your dog’s MBTI type with a personality description, and you can share the result with friends.
