Cat MBTI Test

What is the MBTI personality type?

MBTI is a personality indicator that describes people through 16 types. It reads tendencies from four axes: Extraversion(E)·Introversion(I), Sensing(S)·Intuition(N), Thinking(T)·Feeling(F), and Judging(J)·Perceiving(P). This test playfully applies that idea to your own cat.

Your cat has a personality too

Some cats hide at the sight of a stranger, while others walk right up for a cuddle. Active, laid-back, endlessly curious — every cat has its own behavior and disposition. Picturing how your cat usually acts will lead you to a more accurate result.

How it works

Answer 12 simple questions while picturing your own cat. It takes only a minute or two. At the end you will meet your cat’s MBTI type with a personality description, and you can share the result with friends.

Frequently asked questions

Do cats have an MBTI too?

MBTI is originally a framework for human personality, so cats don't really have one. This test is a playful way to map your cat's everyday behavior onto the 16 MBTI types for fun.

How long does it take?

Just answer 12 simple questions — it usually takes only one or two minutes.

How many result types are there?

Your cat gets one of 16 types — the same 16 as MBTI.

How is my cat's MBTI determined?

Each yes/no answer adds points across four axes — Extraversion(E)·Introversion(I), Sensing(S)·Intuition(N), Thinking(T)·Feeling(F), Judging(J)·Perceiving(P) — and the closest of the 16 types becomes the result.

Can I share the result?

Yes — you can send the result page to friends with the share button.

The 16 cat personality types

Just like the classic MBTI, your cat lands in one of 16 personality types, grouped into four families. Analysts (NT), like the curious ENTP scientist-cat, are clever and independent. Diplomats (NF), such as the imaginative ENFP, are warm and creative. Sentinels (SJ), like the orderly ESTJ “king of the house,” value routine and loyalty. Explorers (SP), such as the show-stealing ESFP, live for play and the spotlight. Take the Cat MBTI Test to see which of the 16 types your cat is.
