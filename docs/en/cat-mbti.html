What is the MBTI personality type?

MBTI is a personality indicator that describes people through 16 types. It reads tendencies from four axes: Extraversion(E)·Introversion(I), Sensing(S)·Intuition(N), Thinking(T)·Feeling(F), and Judging(J)·Perceiving(P). This test playfully applies that idea to your own cat.

Your cat has a personality too

Some cats hide at the sight of a stranger, while others walk right up for a cuddle. Active, laid-back, endlessly curious — every cat has its own behavior and disposition. Picturing how your cat usually acts will lead you to a more accurate result.

How it works

Answer 12 simple questions while picturing your own cat. It takes only a minute or two. At the end you will meet your cat’s MBTI type with a personality description, and you can share the result with friends.
