My Money Type MONEY TYPE

What is the MONEY TYPE money-type test?

MONEY TYPE is a light tendency test that sorts how you grow and keep your money into types. Do you move by sharing info or run things quietly alone? Do you look at track-record data or at growth potential? Do you weigh numbers or trust your gut? Do you save steadily or watch for chances? It mirrors everyone’s different money habits into 16 types — light, fun content, just like MBTI.

How you handle money mirrors who you are

With the same money, one person saves steadily and safely, another bets long on growth potential, another moves fast when a chance appears. How you handle your assets quietly reveals your tendencies and values. Answer honestly about your everyday money habits, and you may meet a side of your financial nature you never noticed. (This test is for fun and is not investment advice.)

How it works

Answer 12 simple questions while recalling your everyday money habits. It takes only a minute or two. At the end you will meet your money type with a description of your tendency and some general money-management tips, and you can share the result with friends.
